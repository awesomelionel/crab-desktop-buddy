What style should the eyes have?

Each style shows the eyes in IDLE state on the 240×135 display. Pick the personality that fits best.

Cartoony

Large circular eyes with iris + pupil + catchlight. Expressive and friendly.

Minimal / Sleek

Wide flat ovals, clean and modern. Easy to animate open/close for blinking.

Robot / Tech

Rounded rectangles with a glowing blue fill. Matches the device's BLE/tech vibe.

All three styles blink periodically in idle state and animate differently per device state.
